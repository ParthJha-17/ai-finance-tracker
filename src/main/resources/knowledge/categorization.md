# Transaction Categorization Knowledge Base

## Housing & Utilities
Use this category for rent, mortgage payments, property taxes, HOA dues, utilities (electricity, water, gas), internet, and mobile plans. Indicators include landlord, lease, utility providers, and recurring monthly bills.

## Transportation
Includes fuel, public transit, rideshare, parking, tolls, vehicle maintenance, car payments, insurance, and registration fees. Keywords: gas, Uber, Lyft, metro, bus, train, parking meter, oil change, tires.

## Groceries
Everyday food and household supplies purchased at supermarkets or grocery stores. Keywords: grocery store, supermarket, produce, pantry, staples.

## Dining & Entertainment
Restaurants, cafes, takeout, bars, streaming services, movies, events, and hobbies. Keywords: restaurant, coffee, bar, Netflix, cinema, concert.

## Healthcare & Insurance
Medical appointments, prescriptions, dental, vision, therapy, and health insurance premiums. Keywords: pharmacy, clinic, copay, premium.

## Personal & Lifestyle
Clothing, grooming, fitness memberships, personal care, and subscriptions not tied to entertainment. Keywords: salon, gym, apparel, skincare.

## Savings & Investments
Transfers to savings accounts, emergency fund, retirement contributions, brokerage investments, and debt payoff beyond minimums. Keywords: transfer to savings, IRA, 401k, brokerage.

## Income
Salary, bonuses, freelance income, refunds, and reimbursements. Keywords: payroll, paycheck, salary, bonus, client payment.

## Education
Tuition, courses, books, certifications, and learning platforms. Keywords: course, tuition, Udemy, certification, textbook.

## Travel
Flights, hotels, vacation rentals, travel insurance, and trip activities. Keywords: airline, hotel, Airbnb, vacation.
